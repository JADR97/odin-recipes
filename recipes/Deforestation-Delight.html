Deforestation Delight

Description

This dish is a real crowd-pleaser, with its bold, earthy flavors and hints of destruction. But be warned: the consequences of indulging in Deforestation Delight are dire.

And let's not forget the impact on our planet's ability to produce oxygen and store carbon, as we continue to burn through our natural resources at an unsustainable pace. So go ahead, take a big bite of Deforestation Delight, but be prepared to pay the price.

Ingredients

  • One large forest
  • Heavy machinery
  • Greed
  • Ignorance

Steps

  1. Take one large, thriving forest and mark it for deforestation.
  2. Bring in heavy machinery and bulldoze through the trees, destroying the natural habitat of countless species.
  3. Ignorantly ignore the crucial role forests play in producing oxygen and storing carbon.
  4. Repeat until all forests are gone, and the planet can no longer support life.
