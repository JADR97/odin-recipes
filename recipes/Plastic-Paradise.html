Plastic Paradise

Description

Looking for a quick and easy way to pollute the planet?

Look no further than Plastic Paradise! With its convenient, single-use packaging and endless array of plastic products, this dish is a favorite among consumers worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Plastic bags, bottles, and other single-use items
  • Convenience
  • Apathy
  • Short-sightedness

Steps

  1. Stock up on plastic bags, bottles, and other single-use items.
  2. Use them freely and without consideration for their environmental impact.
  3. Ignore the fact that plastic waste takes hundreds of years to decompose and harms marine life.
  4. Continue to prioritize convenience over the planet's long-term health.
  5. Watch as our oceans turn into plastic soup and the world suffocates under the weight of our trash.
